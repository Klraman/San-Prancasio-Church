Our Parish Ministry Categories

♥ Liturgical & Sacramental Ministry

Our Liturgical Ministry oversees the celebration of all seven sacraments — from the joyful Baptism of newborns to the sacred Anointing of the Sick. Our trained ministers, lectors, altar servers, and Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion ensure every Mass is celebrated with dignity and reverence. The team coordinates directly with the Parish Priest for all sacramental preparations and scheduling.

♣ Youth & Young Adults Ministry

San Pancracio's Youth Ministry (KYC — Kabataang Yesus Christos) is a vibrant group dedicated to forming young Catholics in faith, service, and community. They organize retreats, recollections, outreach programs, and youth Masses. Every young person in the parish is welcome — from high school students to young professionals — to grow in their faith journey together.

★ Catholic Charismatic Renewal

The Charismatic Renewal community at San Pancracio is one of the most active groups in the parish. They conduct Life in the Spirit Seminars, healing Masses, prayer meetings, and home prayer gatherings. Known for their joyful and Spirit-filled worship, they are at the forefront of evangelization and parish renewal efforts throughout Caloocan.

⌂ Basic Ecclesial Communities (BEC)

The BEC structure divides the parish into smaller neighborhood faith communities, each led by a lay coordinator. These communities hold block rosaries, Bible sharing sessions, and community meetings. They are the backbone of the parish's pastoral work — bringing the Church directly into the homes and streets of Grace Park, Caloocan.

✨ Caritas & Social Action Ministry

Inspired by Catholic Social Teaching, our Caritas ministry reaches out to the poor and marginalized in the La Loma area. They organize feeding programs, medical missions, scholarship assistance, and relief operations during calamities. The ministry works in partnership with the Diocese of Kalookan's social action office and various civic organizations.
