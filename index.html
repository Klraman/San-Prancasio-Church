Welcome to San Pancracio Parish

Established on October 11, 1962 by the late Cardinal Rufino Santos, San Pancracio Parish stands as a beacon of faith within the La Loma Cemetery Compound, Grace Park, Caloocan City. Under the Diocese of Kalookan and the Vicariate of Our Lady of Grace, we proudly serve a community of over 57,000 faithful — united in prayer, worship, and love for God and neighbor. Our mission is to bring the Church closer to the people through active ministry, sacramental life, and community outreach.

☥

Our Mission

To nurture a living community of faith through the sacraments, prayer, and active service — bringing the Gospel to every home in Caloocan.

🙏

Our Community

With 14 lay associations and a vibrant Charismatic core group, San Pancracio Parish is home to a diverse and dedicated family of believers.
